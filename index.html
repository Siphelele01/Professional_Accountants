Our Services

We provide accurate, professional, and cost-effective services that drive business growth and success. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional financial solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Financial Reporting

  • Compilation of Annual Financial Statements
  • Bookkeeping to trial balance
  • Bank and petty cash reconciliation
  • Creditors and Debtors reconciliation

Tax Services

  • Company and individual compliance
  • Personal income tax returns and corporate returns
  • Provisional tax calculation and submission
  • Declared and deemed dividends
  • Tax consultations and advice

Payroll Management

  • Monthly payroll processing
  • Payslip preparation and issuing
  • IRP5s/IT3s certificates creation
  • Employer salary recons (EMP501)
  • Workman's compensation return of earnings

Compliance Services

  • Filing of CIPC mandatory Annual returns
  • Tax Compliance Pins application
  • BEE Affidavits issuing
  • Objections to SARS completion and submission
  • Tax directives application

Registration Services

  • Company registration with CIPC and SARS
  • Department of Labour & Compensation Commissioner
  • VAT, PAYE, UIF, and SDL Registrations
  • Company profile amendments
  • Employee registration and removal

Additional Services

  • Company de-registration with CIPC and SARS
  • Director changes and company conversions
  • Address changes
  • Personal income tax number registration
  • Tax number retrieval
